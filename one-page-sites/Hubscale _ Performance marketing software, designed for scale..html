Hubscale was originally created to help our team best address the challenges of performance marketing. Now we bring it to you

Intuitive and Efficient

Our user-friendly interface was designed by optimizing every single step of the campaign management workflow in order to save you precious time and efforts

Automated

From campaign optimization to status notifications and internal communication, most of your team’s daily tasks have been automatized to mitigate workload and enhance productivity

Flexible

With its extensive variety of advanced options, our platform allows you to create both simple and sophisticated campaigns to ensure all your performance marketing needs are met

Robust and Stable

Hubscale’s infrastructure is based on RTB standards and can therefore handle large quantities of data in real-time, without compromising stability and scalability

Features

AUTO-OPTIMIZATION

Define your KPIs and let the system optimize your campaigns automatically, 24/7.

FRAUD PREVENTION

Use our built-in fraud detection algorithms or create your own rules to flag and automatically reject fraudulent activity before it impacts your budget and performance.

PUBLISHER SCORING

Leverage our recommendation tool to identify the publishers who best fit your campaigns according to previous performance and attribute tagging.

EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS

Communicate smoothly with your publishers and inform them of any changes in real-time, directly from our platform and using your own email servers.

ACTIONABLE DASHBOARD

From a broad overview to the most detailed performance insights, easily access the information you need and instantly implement your optimization decisions with a single click.

SMART ANALYTICS

Take advantage of the powerful capabilities of our reporting engine to monitor and analyze your data using both simple and highly-complex filters.

ADVANCED API

Automate workflows using our pre-integrated two-way API: seamlessly pull campaigns from your advertisers and automatically push them to your publishers based on customizable rules.

MULTI-EVENT TRACKING

Control the quality of your traffic in real-time: track up to 10 post-conversion events and decide which ones to share with your publishers.

ABOUT US

We are striving to make performance marketing better

Hubscale.io was developed by the founders of Adxperience, a tier-one ad-tech agency that has been helping many brands and app developers worldwide achieve campaign success since 2012. Their years of campaign management experience and technical know-how were compiled into one powerful software, offering an unparalleled set of features and capabilities. Today, we are proud to release this proprietary technology to the market, making Hubscale.io available to professionals looking to take their desktop or mobile performance marketing to the next level.
